Caring for someone who has a medical issue can be hard to deal with. It can be upsetting and difficult to see them struggle if you are a carer or a family member. The weight of caregiving have a wide impact on friends, family members and carers and we want to support them to help them care better, live their own life, and develop coping mechanisms. The family support group is not about what the person is going through, but about how the family and loved ones are coping.
